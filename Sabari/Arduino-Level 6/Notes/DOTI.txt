DOTI - Display Output Trini Interface
DOTI is connected to PORTC
DOTI consists of Buzzer, trimpot, SSD(Seven Segment Display)
and LCD pinouts

SSD - Seven Segment Display:
SSD are classified as Common Anode SSD and Common Cathode SSD

Common Anode SSD:
-All the anodes in the LEDs are made common(shorter). To glow a segment just need to connect the negative part

Common Cathode SSD:
-All the cathodes in the LEDs are made common(shorter). To glow a segment just need to connect the positive part
