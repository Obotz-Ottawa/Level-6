ATMega324

Features of ATMega324:

1) It is a 8-bit microcontroller
2) It has 32 KB ROM and 2KB Ram
3) The maximum clock frequency is 16 MHz
4) It has 4 bi directional ports; 32 i/o pins
5) It has 8 ADC pins
6) ATMega 324 has 3 timers and 32 general working registers
